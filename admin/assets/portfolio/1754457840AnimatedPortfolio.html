About Me

Motion Designer & Developer

Passionate about creating seamless user experiences through thoughtful animation and interaction design.

I'm Maya Chen, an Animation UI/UX Developer with over 4 years of experience creating engaging digital experiences. I specialize in bringing static designs to life through meaningful animations and micro-interactions.

My expertise lies in bridging the gap between design and development, ensuring that every animation serves a purpose in enhancing user experience. I work with modern tools and frameworks to create performant, accessible animations.

When I'm not animating interfaces, you can find me exploring new animation techniques, contributing to open-source projects, or teaching motion design workshops.
